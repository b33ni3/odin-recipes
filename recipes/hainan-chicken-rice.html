Hainan Chicken and Rice Recipe

This dish is a staple in Malaysia and Singapore.

It's so simple, yet very delicious. The best version of chicken and rice in the world.

Ingredients

Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • kosher salt to taste
  • water to cover
  • 4 inch knob ginger
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 1.5 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 oz dried shiitake

Rice:

  • 2.5 tbsp vegetable oil
  • excess fast from earlier, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 3 inch knob ginger, minced
  • Chicken skin of two thighs
  • 2 cups WASHED rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth from earlier
